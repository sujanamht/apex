APEX Worldwide Express

Since 1995, APEX Worldwide Express has been more than just a courier service—we are your trusted global logistics partner. What began as a passionate family business in Nepal has grown into an international network spanning India, Singapore, and Nepal, offering reliable, secure, and personalized delivery solutions.
With a commitment to efficiency, transparency, and innovation, we tailor logistics strategies to meet your specific needs—whether it's urgent documents, sensitive cargo, or oversized freight. Backed by cutting-edge technology and a human-centered approach, we ensure seamless, stress-free shipping so you can focus on what matters most. Let APEX handle your logistics with unwavering reliability and personalized service—because your shipment is more than just a package; it's a priority.
