ABOUT US

APEX Worldwide Express

Since 1995, APEX Worldwide Express has been a trusted name in global logistics, providing secure, reliable, and customized shipping solutions. What started as a family-driven business in Nepal has expanded into an extensive international network, with key operations in India, Singapore, and beyond. Our strength lies in combining global reach with local expertise, ensuring smooth and efficient air, sea, and road freight services tailored to the diverse needs of individuals and businesses. With a deep understanding of international logistics and regulations, we navigate complex shipping processes with ease, offering cost-effective, high-security, and timely deliveries across the world. We believe in complete transparency and seamless communication, keeping our customers informed with real-time tracking and proactive updates, ensuring that every shipment is handled with the utmost care and precision.

At APEX, we are driven by innovation and customer satisfaction. Our cutting-edge technology enhances security, efficiency, and visibility, but we never lose sight of the human connection that builds long-term trust. We take pride in our unwavering reliability, ensuring that every shipment, no matter how complex, reaches its destination on time and intact. More than just a logistics provider, we view ourselves as partners to our customers, delivering personalized service that goes beyond expectations. Additionally, we are committed to sustainability, minimizing our environmental impact through eco-friendly logistics practices, optimized transport routes, and responsible operations.

At APEX Worldwide Express, we don’t just move shipments—we connect businesses, bridge borders, and deliver trust with every package we handle.
